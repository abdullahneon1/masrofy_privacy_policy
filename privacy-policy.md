Privacy Policy for Masrofy
1. Data Collection & Use
Masrofy is a local-first application designed to give you full control over your finances.

Financial Records: All expense and income records you enter are stored directly on your device. We do not store your personal financial data on our servers.

SMS Data (New): With your explicit consent, Masrofy accesses your SMS messages to identify transaction notifications from financial institutions. This data is used solely to automate expense logging and is never used for marketing or profiling.

2. Sensitive Permissions
We only request permissions absolutely necessary for our core features:

SMS (READ_SMS): Used to identify and parse bank transaction messages for automatic logging.

Camera: Used solely for scanning physical receipts to extract date, amount, and vendor data.

Microphone: Used only for voice-to-text expense entry through our "Smart Voice" feature.

3. Third-Party Processing & AI
To provide "Smart" features, Masrofy utilizes third-party AI services:

Groq Cloud AI: Snippets of text from your bank SMS, voice transcripts, and receipt data are sent securely to Groq Cloud for analysis. This data is processed in real-time to extract expense details and is not stored or used to train AI models by Masrofy.

Google ML Kit: We use local on-device machine learning for initial text recognition from camera images.

4. Data Sharing & Security
We prioritize your privacy above all else.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or transaction history with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data sent to third-party AI (Groq) is anonymized as much as possible; we do not send your name, phone number, or full account numbers to the AI.

5. User Control
You can revoke SMS, Camera, or Microphone permissions at any time through your device settings. If you choose not to grant these permissions, Masrofy remains fully functional via manual entry.
