Privacy Policy for Masrofy

1. Data Collection: Masrofy is a local-first application. We do not collect or store your personal financial data on our servers. All expense records are stored directly on your device.

2. Permissions: > * Camera: Used solely for scanning receipts to extract expense data.

Microphone: Used only for voice-to-text expense entry.

3. Third-Party Services: We use Groq Cloud AI for processing voice and receipt text. Your voice data and receipt images are sent securely to Groq for analysis but are not stored by Masrofy. We also use Google ML Kit for local text recognition.

4. Data Security: We prioritize your privacy and do not sell or share any user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Contact: For questions, contact neonstudioseg@gmail.com.
